Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • Salt, pepper, herbs

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Sauté onion and beef until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and seasonings, simmer 15 min.
  4. Combine with spaghetti and serve hot.

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Whisk wet ingredients in another.
  3. Combine and cook on a hot pan until bubbles form.
  4. Flip and cook until golden brown. Serve warm.
